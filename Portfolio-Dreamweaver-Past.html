Here's my Athletic Past:

It all started one faithful morning my freshman year of high school when I decided to go on a run... I started from square one: hadn't broken 10 minutes in the mile run, and couldn't even run half a mile without stopping to walk. After over a year of hard and persistent effort, I had made it on to the Varisty track and cross country team at my High School, which launched a love for running and endurance. A foot/ankle injury in early 2017 took me out of running for 3.5 years. For a while, I was lost and didn't know what to do with myself. But over time, I came to try swimming, cycling, I started Nordic skiing in college with no prior experience, and joined a rowing club as well! It was a blessing in disguise -- without it, I would never have branched out to try so many new sports and met so many amazing people along the way. Now, I'm getting back into running and taking it carefully.
